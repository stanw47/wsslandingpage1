About Us

Williamson Security Solutions (WSS) is a cybersecurity-focused consulting, education, and SaaS provider based in Maryland, USA. Our mission is to empower businesses with accessible, reliable, and cutting-edge cybersecurity tools that protect sensitive data, simplify compliance, and foster trust in a digital-first world.

Our Flagship Products

WebSecTester

An automated security testing tool that performs penetration testing, exposes potential PII leaks, and simulates attacks to assess vulnerabilities in web applications.

WebSecAudit

A platform for managing and tracking audit processes. It guides organizations through compliance requirements, offers step-by-step instructions, and facilitates manual or automated proof reviews.
